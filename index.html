How to Make mac and chesee

Ingridients

  • macaroni
  • full cream milk
  • Mushroom broth
  • chedar chesee
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • sausage or any kind of meat
  • corn starch
  • black pepper(optional)
  • rosemary and basil(optional)
  • mozzarela
  • oil

Steps

  1. put water on the pot and turn on the stove.
  2. And then put salt and oil.
  3. when it boil put macaroni in to the pot after it'sa cooked properly turn off the stove and drained the macaroni.
  4. After that, put macaroni in the bowl.
  5. for the sauce put full cream milk in the pot and turn on the stove
  6. put your chedar chesee and mozzarela in to the pot and stirr untill it melt.
  7. after it melt put your Mushroom broth, salt, peper, and black pepper inside mix it until it's combined.
  8. mix your corn starch with water and put it in to the milk and then you can put your meat.
  9. After the sauce is done you can put your pasta in to the sauce and mixid.
  10. after that put your rosemary and basil on top.
  11. and the finnal steps put your macaroni in the serving plate.
